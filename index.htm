About PaySprint

"PaySprint Inc. is a leading financial technology company based in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. Established in 2021, our mission is to enhance financial inclusion for underserved communities in urban and semi-urban areas through our innovative product, PaySprint.

Our solutions
QR Code for Face-to-Face Payments

An affordable alternative to POS terminals, QR codes eliminate transaction costs and technical issues, allowing businesses to receive payments directly into their accounts without usage or transaction fees.

Payment Links for Remote Customers

Easily shareable links for remote payments, embedded in various online messages for convenience.

Virtual Payment Terminal

Enables merchants to receive payments over the phone by processing card details directly into their accounts

API for Online Payments

Simple integration for accepting payments on business websites, essential for successful eCommerce operations.

Payment Processing solutions
eCommerce Solution
Our business accounts include comprehensive eCommerce solutions that enable merchants to take online orders, sell on social media, offer loyalty programs, own site domains, and utilize various other features to enhance their online presence.
Store-Front Solution
Our storefront solutions provide merchants with the ability to sell products or solutions in person using their devices, saving on POS system costs. Additional features include customer information management, marketing campaigns, and inventory management.
